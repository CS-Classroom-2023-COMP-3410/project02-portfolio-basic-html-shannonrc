 My Story

I’m Shannon! I am from California, a Computer Science, EDPX, and Psychology student at the University of Denver. I enjoy learning how people interact with systems and how design choices psychologically impact everyday experiences.

Outside of academics, I have a strong interest in film and media, having grown up around the film industry through my dad. I enjoy watching movies, listening to music, and traveling to learn about different cultures and languages. I also play and coach ice hockey and volleyball.

Study Abroad

Studying abroad in South Korea was an incredibly meaningful and eye opening experience. Being immersed in a new culture pushed me to observe how design, communication, and everyday systems differ across countries. I became especially interested in Korean fashion and beauty/skincare, which led me to notice how thoughtfully user experience is integrated into daily life, branding, and technology. Living abroad helped me grow personally and culturally, allowing me to learn more about myself and how I interact with others. I made amazing friends from many different countries, and those relationships, along with the experience itself, reshaped how I think about design, culture, and global perspectives.
